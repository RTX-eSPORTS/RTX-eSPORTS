Rules for RTX eSports Paid Scrims

  • No revive allowed
  • No refund to eliminated players
  • If player can't join at right time, then they will be eliminated
  • If player leaves the match, then they will be eliminated
  • Gun attributes are not allowed
  • Random maps
  • Game modes are of choice [CS or BR]
  • All players have to enroll first
  • All details must be provided as requested to enroll
  • First payment then enrollment
  • Minimum team (or Players) to start match is four
  • Room ID and password uploaded 5 to 10 minutes before match on WhatsApp Channel and Announcement
  • Team up not allowed
  • Multiple accounts not allowed
  • Hack, Panel, PC, Emulator or any other third-party apps or services are not allowed
  • All participants must register before the match
  • Slots are limited; first come first serve
  • Teams must follow the scheduled match timing
  • No cheating or use of unauthorized software/hacks
  • Respect other players and organizers
  • Disqualification may occur if rules are violated
  • Matches are subject to referee decisions which are final
  • Maintain proper communication in team chats
  • Ensure a stable internet connection during matches
  • Any disputes should be reported immediately to the help desk
